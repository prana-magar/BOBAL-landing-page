Mnemonics Flashcard

Helps you learn and remember new IELTS words

BOBAL provides flashcards with frequently asked IELTS and GRE vocabulary. The flashcards also contains Mnemonics that help you retain what you learned and makes learning fun.

Our adaptive algorithm will help you learn words that you are finding difficult to remember and makes surre you aren't wasting your time in words you already know.

Learning with "CONTEXT"

Helps you learn words in context with actual news articles

Learning vocabulary with context is very important for IELTS reading and writing. BOBAL provides various recent news articles that contains IELTS words. Everyday you get new articles at your finger tips.

BOBAL uses advance search engine and allows you to search any IELTS word, ultimately returning recent news articles in the domain that interests you like politics, sports, music, science etc.
